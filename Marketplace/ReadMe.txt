Text Templator v1.0

WHAT IS IT?
This app lets you create text templates that you might want to put as email/SMS/other and have them copied straight into your clipboard.

WHAT'S COOL?
You can pin each template to your start screen, tapping that icon will copy that particular template straight into your clipboard with no fuss
